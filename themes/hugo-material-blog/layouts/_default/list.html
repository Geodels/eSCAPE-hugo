What is it?
eSCAPE is a scalable parallelised Python-based numerical model built to simulate landscapes and basins evolution at high resolution.
For what purpose?
eSCAPE can address problems related to Earth surface dynamics such as geomorphology, hydrology, and stratigraphy, it can also be used in related fields.
